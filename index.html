Find the Impostor

How to play

At the beginning of each round, every player receives a question. One player's question will be different from the group — he is the impostor.

Your objective as a player is to deduce the impostor and convince other players to vote him out.

Impostor's objective is to convince the rest of the party that he got the same question as everyone else and persuade them to vote for another player. If the impostor is still in the game after voting, he wins.

Локальна HTML5-гра: кожен раунд автоматично отримує нову випадкову тему та пару питань. Питання приховані до кінця раунду.
